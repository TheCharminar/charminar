Significance of the Charminar Today

The Charminar today serves as one of the most prominent pillars of the culture, religion and society of Hyderabad. Not only is the mosque on the second floor operational for daily worship purposes, but it attracts huge number of residents and tourits in the land of India.

When important religious fesitvals such as Eid occur, it is thus apparent that the monument not only stands as a relic but also continues its role as a place of religious significance and spiritual activity.

The markets surrounding the Charminar remain just as busy and economically thriving as they did centuries ago. Local vendors offer tourists all types of traditional items including bangles, jewelry, clothes, and even food. In addition to this, the monument also serves as one of the central meeting places in the city for travelers and residents alike. As such, it continues to be a strong symbol of the identity of Hyderabad.
